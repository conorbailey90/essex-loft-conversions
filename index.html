Hip to Gable Conversions

These are the cheapest way of converting your loft space. They are also the easiest and least disruptive conversions to complete. They do, however, provide the least amount of extra space due to the head height. But this is ideal for a bedroom conversion. A Velux loft conversion in Essex can cost between £12,000-£20,000 +VAT


With a dormer loft conversion, you will be increasing the amount of headspace available. This is the 2nd cheapest way of converting your loft space, a dormer conversion protrudes from the roofline. These are more expensive than a velux conversion, but they will expand the amount of headspace unlike a velux conversion, this makes them suitable for pretty much any home. The average cost for Dormer loft conversion in Essex is £30,000-£65,000 depending on finishes.


A hip to gable loft conversion is where the existing shape of the roof is changed. The top line of the roof is extended so that it runs all the way to the edge of the house. The end result is that instead of having a sloping roof on the side of the house, there will now be a vertical gable that can either be made of brick or tile. This creates a lot of extra space. It only works in a semi-detached or detached property with a sloping roof. With a detached home, you can convert both sides to create a double hip-to-gable conversion. The average cost for a hip to gable loft conversion in Essex is: £40,000-£70,000.


With a mansard loft conversion the whole shape of the roof is changed. The roof is made to be flat and the new walls come up at oround 75 degrees. They will appear almost vertical from the outside. In most cases, dormer windows will also be added. This will give the most space but will also be the most expensive option. The average cost for a mansard conversion in Essex is £45,000-£85,000.
